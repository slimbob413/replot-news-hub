About Replot News

Replot News is Nigeria's first AI-powered news platform, launched in 2025. It uses advanced artificial intelligence to rewrite breaking news from multiple credible Nigerian and international sources, delivering stories with maximum clarity and factual accuracy.

How Replot News Works

Replot News aggregates stories from major Nigerian news outlets including Punch, Vanguard, Premium Times, The Guardian Nigeria, The Cable, and Channels TV. Each story is then processed through an AI system that:

  • Verifies claims against multiple sources
  • Rewrites the narrative for clarity and readability
  • Assigns factuality scores based on source agreement
  • Categorizes stories into politics, business, technology, sports, and entertainment

Key Features

AI-Powered Rewriting: Every article on Replot News is rewritten by AI for clarity while maintaining factual accuracy.
Multi-Source Verification: Stories are cross-referenced across multiple credible news sources.
Trending Analysis: The trending page tracks what Nigerians are discussing on X (Twitter) and Google Trends.
RSS Feed: Subscribe to the Replot News RSS feed for real-time updates.
SEO-Optimized: Structured data, fast load times, and mobile-first design for the best reading experience.

Technical Stack

Replot News is built using modern web technologies: Next.js for server-side rendering, Prisma for data management, and DeepSeek AI for intelligent content processing. The platform delivers sub-second page loads and full mobile responsiveness.
